My Hobbies

  • Hand Taming Birds

    I love spending time with birds, especially hand-taming them. It’s a rewarding experience to develop trust and build a bond with these beautiful creatures. Over time, I have trained them to interact with me and feel comfortable on my hand.

  • Painting

    Painting is my creative escape. Whether it’s watercolors, acrylics, or oils, I enjoy experimenting with colors and textures to create both abstract and realistic art. It helps me relax and express my emotions in a visual form.

  • Playing with Kids

    Spending time with kids brings joy and laughter to my life. I enjoy playing games, teaching them new things, and just having fun together. It’s amazing how their energy and curiosity can brighten up any day!

  • Reading Books

    Reading is one of my favorite hobbies, allowing me to escape into different worlds and expand my knowledge. Whether it’s fiction, non-fiction, or self-improvement books, I always find new insights to improve myself.
